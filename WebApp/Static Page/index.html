What is WILDLIFE TRACKER?

A Researching System Specialy Designed For Wildlife Researchers

Are you a wildlife researcher who wants a efficent and safe method to do your researchs?
This system could be the solution for your problems.

  Our wildlife tracking system provides many features to conduct research on wild animals very effectively and safely.The main feature of our system is it provides facilities to analyse the behaviour of wild animals remotely. We have a tracking device that can be operated in any harsh environment and a web application to analyze data observed by these devices remotely. The owner of a particular device can access these observations of devices from anywhere in the world. You don't have to stay in your researching area throughout the process anymore.
  Usually, wild animals do not behave as usual when there are human activities around. It is a critical problem that many researchers are facing. Predator attacks are another concern of wildlife researchers. You don't have to worry about these things anymore. Since the researcher can monitor the devices remotely, it reduces the human activities in researching area and reduce the probability of being a victim to a predator attack.

AWESOME FEATURES

Here are the wonderful features of our tracking system.....

Remote Monitoring

Owner of a device can monitor the observations of a device using the web Application.

GPRS Connectivity

Device can be used in wild areas that doesn't have WiFi access. Device can operate with GPRS signals.

Data Security

Only the user of a particular device can refer the data observed by that device throgh the web app.

Scalability

One user can have one or more devices. They can place them in different places and connect with his profile and monitor all devices at once.

Realtime Images

The sensor of the device can detect animals withn 7m 120deg range.It will trigger the camera to take realtime pictures and upload to cloud.

Automatic Flash

The device has a flash light.In low light conditions this flash will automatically turn on.The device can take clear photos in any time at a day.

Video Recording

After capture the photos the device records a video clip.This video is recorded while the animal is in sensing range. Then this video is stored in the device storage.Videos are not visible in web app.

Self Storage

The device is capable of having a own storage. Owner of a device can insert a micro SD card to the device. When there is no internet connectivity. Device will automatically write all the data to SD card.

Location Tracker

The device contains a GPS tracker. User can see where the devices are exactly located in using the map provided in web app. If user have multiple devices, all the device locations will be pointed out in the map.

Rechargable

The device is powered with by rechragable batteries.

Solar Power

The device has a solar cell. The rechragable batteries are charged using this solar cell. Also, device can be operate using solar power.

Notification Service

The notification service of our system is notifying the owner of a device by a SMS when it detect animal.

Registration

First of all, you have to register in our web application.You can register in our system using the link provided below.

In this step you have to provide a valid letter, ID card or any document to prove that you are a wildlife researcher. We are only allowing you to use our services if the data you entered are valid and the document you submited is sufficent to recognize that you are a wildlife researcher. If your request is accepted by us we will notify you. Then you can move into the next step.

Register Now
Connect Device

If your request to our system is accepted. You can buy a devices and connect them with your profile.

Every device has its own ID and password.When you buy device these values will be in the manual provided with the device. You have to use that password and ID to connect your device to your profile. Log in to your profile and there will be a tab called 'Device'.Enter relevent password and ID there to connect the device. Then you can analyse all the observations from that device from your profile. If you have multiple devices you have to follow this step for each device.

Login Now
Setup A Device

You have to insert a sim card with a data plan and credits to your device to have GPRS connectivity. Make sure that this sim card is not the sim card that holds the phone number that you have entered when you are registering. Also, you have to insert a SD card. The device supports any type of microSD cards upto 128GB.

Then you can place your device in your researching area. We are recommending you to place your device so that, the sensor is faced to pathway that you are expecting animals will roam. Sensor can detect animals within 7m and 120deg range. Also, you have to place the solar panel so that sun light is directly fall on to its surface. After this you can monitor the device from the web app.

All Set

Congratulations!!!!!!! At this point all the pre configurations are done.

Now you can analyse the photos captured by your devices from your profile. You can see the photos under the photos tab. If you want to see the locations, you can see them under location tab.
